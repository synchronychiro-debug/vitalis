# 01 — Product Vision & Target Users

## Mission

Build the definitive practice management platform for animal chiropractors — one that respects the realities of how practitioners actually work (mobile, multi-species, often solo or small-team) and gives them tools that match the quality of care they deliver.

## Why Animals-Only

By focusing exclusively on animal patients:
- **No HIPAA compliance burden** — reduces engineering cost and regulatory risk substantially
- **Faster iteration** — fewer audit and certification gates
- **Underserved market** — most veterinary EHRs are built for traditional vet medicine and do not handle chiropractic workflows well
- **Pricing flexibility** — lower compliance overhead = lower SaaS pricing

## Primary Personas

### 1. The Mobile Solo Practitioner (e.g., Dr. Philip)
- Travels between barns, homes, and clinics
- Sees mixed species in a single day (dogs, horses)
- Needs offline access in rural areas
- Books appointments through requests, not direct scheduling
- Uses an iPad as primary device
- Needs fast note entry between appointments

### 2. The Brick-and-Mortar Clinic Practitioner
- Works in a fixed location with set hours
- Often part of a multi-provider practice
- Needs front-desk-friendly scheduling
- Uses a desktop computer for documentation
- May employ admin and support staff
- Direct scheduling is preferred over requests

### 3. The Multi-Provider Practice Owner / Admin
- Manages 2–10 providers
- Tracks revenue and performance per provider
- Sets up CRM and marketing automations
- Controls system-wide settings and permissions
- Needs reporting for business decisions

### 4. The Client (Animal Owner)
- Has 1–5 animals under care
- Wants to see notes, history, and bills in one place
- Wants to book or request appointments without phone tag
- Wants to pay online
- Wants to know how their animal is progressing

## Success Metrics (Commercial)

- 100 paying clinics within 18 months of public launch
- $50–$200/month per provider seat (tiered)
- <5% monthly churn
- Net Promoter Score >50 among active users
- Synchrony Chiropractic eliminates reliance on all third-party EHR/PM tools within 90 days of Phase 3 completion

## Non-Goals

- Human medical patients (HIPAA)
- Veterinary surgical or laboratory workflows (out of scope; integration only)
- Inventory management for retail products (deferred to post-v1)
- Insurance claim processing (deferred to post-v1)
